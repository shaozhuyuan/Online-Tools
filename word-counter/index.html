Advanced Word Counter: Writing metrics and SEO keyword density

For bloggers, students, and SEO marketers, tracking the length of essays or articles is critical. A secure online word counter helps you instantly check counts without risking leakage of draft ideas.

How Reading and Speaking Times are Calculated:

The standard reading speed for most adults is roughly 200 words per minute (WPM). Speaking speed for speeches or presentations averages around 130 WPM. The counter uses these factors to estimate speech durations.

Keyword Density Importance:

In search engine optimization, keyword density represents the percentage of times a specific keyword appears within a text body. Maintaining a natural density of 1% to 2% is ideal for search indexing compliance.
