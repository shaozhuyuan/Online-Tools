Color formats explained: HEX, RGB, HSL, and CMYK

Understanding color spaces is essential for digital designers and developers. An online color picker & converter provides cross-format translation between display and print standards.

RGB vs. HSL:

RGB (Red, Green, Blue) represents color as it is emitted by electronic displays. HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) describes colors in a way that matches human perception, making it easier to adjust shading.

What is WCAG Contrast?

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) specify that text on a background must maintain a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 (for normal text) to ensure readability for visually impaired users.
